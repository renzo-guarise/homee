Parametric analysis of a three-degree system of freedom

This project analyzes the response of a three-story portico to an excitement at its base, similar to that of the Caucete earthquake. For this, the system was simplified to three degrees of freedom, where each one represented a floor, and then a parametric study was made based on different damping ratios.
